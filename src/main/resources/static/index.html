With each new quarter students may have trouble connecting with new classmates, and consequently finding suitable study groups or study partners may be challenging. Additionally, factors such as compatible schedules and similar study habits or preferences need to be taken into account in order to ensure a pleasant and productive experience for each individual.

Proposed Solution:

This can be accomplished be developing a collaborative filtering system where users can create profiles with their current schedule, classes, preferred study times, place, and any other user-specific preferences. The system will then process and recommend study partners based on the available user information and their study history.
